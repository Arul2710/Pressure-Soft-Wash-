Frequently Asked Questions

How long does preservation take?

Usually, the entire pressing and drying process takes 4-6 weeks to ensure colors are perfectly retained without moisture.

Can I send dried flowers?

Yes, we can work with pre-dried flowers for certain resin art and frame layouts, though fresh is always best for standard pressing.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping for all our botanical frames and resin pieces with secure, moisture-proof packaging.

How should I care for my artwork?

Keep your frame away from direct sunlight to prevent fading. A dry, cool place is perfect for your botanical treasures.
